### Early years
Went to kindergarden in Baltimore
Developed accute tortacollis around 3 years old, was hospitalized with traction, in Jewish Hospital of New York for 1 month
eventually my neck adjusted itsself
Parent’s divorced when I was around 5 years old. 
I went to stay with my grandparents in Brookly, New York
I entered public school (P.S. 93) and attended grades 1 - 4 there, I excelled in reading and science, having an above average reading level
My grandparents read a lot. There were always, books and magazines and newspapers around
My grandparents read the newspaper every day.
We had television and watched TV shows and always the evening news.
My Great Uncle Milton Murray gave me a full  set of Encyclopedia Britannica (1957) when I was 6 or 7 years old,
I would “browse” it for hours going from subject to subject, following links.

#### Spiritual development and interests
Grandmother taught me to say my prayers every night, the Lord’s Prayer and the 23rd Psalm
She sent me to 1st church AME Zion church around the corner for sunday school. Where I eventually attended 
catechism class and was confirmed.
I read a lot of comic books.
Around 12, I became interested in “The Occult”, read a book called “The Black Arts” Also books on Astrology and Numerology.
In 4th grade Mrs. Chandler took us on a lot of field trips around NYC, this broadened my horizons considerably.
Pivotal moment sitting in the UN General Assembly, I became a “World Citizen” at that moment.
We also had a field trip to the 1965 New York World’s Fair.Talked on a picture phone (Bell Telephone exhibit) These trpis turned me into a futurist.
